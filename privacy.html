1. Introduction

Abaris Healthcare Pvt Ltd respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard data when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, etc. (when you fill contact forms or inquiries).
  • Non-Personal Information: Browser type, IP address, device details, and browsing activity for analytical purposes.

3. How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide requested information.
  • To improve website performance and user experience.
  • To send relevant updates, if you have opted to receive them.
  • To comply with regulatory and legal obligations.

4. Data Protection & Security

  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information.
  • Our practices comply with applicable data protection laws in India and international guidelines where required.
  • However, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or trade personal information. We may share data only:

  • With trusted service providers assisting us in website operations.
  • When required by law, regulation, or government authority.
  • To meet compliance obligations with pharmaceutical regulatory bodies.

6. Cookies & Tracking

Our Site may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance user experience and analyze site traffic. You may disable cookies in your browser settings if preferred.

7. Third-Party Links

This Site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of such third parties.

8. Children’s Privacy

This website is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Your Rights

Depending on applicable data protection laws, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available on this Site.
