Commitment to Our Partners

  • Raw Material Testing

    Every raw ingredient undergoes strict identity, purity, and potency tests before approval, ensuring only the finest materials enter our production lines.

  • In-Process Quality Control (IPQC)

    Real-time monitoring during formulation, mixing, filling, and packaging ensures each batch meets precise specifications continuously, preventing defects early.

  • Finished Product Testing

    Comprehensive lab analyses confirm that every medicine meets our standards for strength, stability, sterility, and safety before release.
