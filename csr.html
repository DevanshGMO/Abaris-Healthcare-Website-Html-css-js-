Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

We are committed to conducting business in a socially responsible and ethical manner minimizing environmental impact, supporting communities, and operating with transparency.

Scope. This policy applies to all Abaris Healthcare employees and contractors.

At a glance

Ethical Conduct Environmental Sustainability Community Engagement Employee Volunteering Philanthropy Transparent Reporting

CSR Policy

Purpose Scope Principles Implementation Compliance Communication Resources Review & Adoption

Purpose

The purpose of this Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Policy is to outline Abaris Healthcare’s commitment to operate ethically, contribute positively to the communities and environments in which we work, and support sustainable development.

This summarizes the “Purpose” section of the official CSR policy.

Scope

This policy applies to all employees and contractors associated with Abaris Healthcare.

Our CSR Principles

Ethical Conduct

We uphold integrity, transparency, and ethical behaviour in all operations and interactions.

Environmental Sustainability

We work to minimize our environmental impact by conserving resources and adopting eco-friendly practices and technologies.

Community Engagement

We actively engage with communities, supporting local initiatives, partnerships, and social welfare programs.

Employee Volunteering

We encourage and support employees to participate in volunteer activities and community service.

Philanthropy

We allocate resources toward reputable charitable causes aligned with our values.

How We Implement CSR

CSR Committee Oversight
A cross-functional CSR Committee oversees implementation and periodic review of this policy.
Integrated into Business Strategy
CSR considerations are embedded into decision-making and day-to-day operations across the company.
Reporting & Transparency
We provide regular updates on CSR activities and performance to stakeholders to ensure accountability.

Compliance & Continuous Improvement

We comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and international standards related to CSR in every jurisdiction where we operate. We continually evaluate and improve our initiatives, policies, and performance based on feedback and best practices.

Communication

Internal Communication

We share CSR objectives, initiatives, and achievements to build awareness and a culture of responsibility among employees.

External Communication

We engage with customers, suppliers, partners, and communities to share CSR information and invite collaboration.

Resources

Adequate financial, human, and technological resources are allocated to support effective CSR initiatives.

Review & Adoption

This CSR Policy is reviewed periodically and updated to reflect evolving expectations and business priorities. The policy is formally adopted and endorsed by Abaris Healthcare leadership.
