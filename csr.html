Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

We are committed to conducting business in a socially responsible and ethical manner minimizing environmental impact, supporting communities, and operating with transparency.

CSR Policy
Purpose Scope Principles Implementation Compliance Communication Resources Review & Update Adoption
1. Purposeon

The purpose of this Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Policy is to outline ABARIS HEALTHCARE PVT. LTD.'s commitment to conducting business in a socially responsible and ethical manner. This policy aims to define our approach towards contributing positively to the communities and environments in which we operate, thereby enhancing sustainable development.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees and contractors associated with ABARIS HEALTHCARE PVT. LTD. It encompasses our commitment to ethical business practices, community engagement, environmental sustainability, and corporate philanthropy.

3. Principles
Ethical Conduct

We uphold the highest standards of integrity, transparency, and ethical behaviour in all our operations and interactions.

Environmental Sustainability

We are committed to minimizing our environmental impact by promoting sustainable practices, reducing carbon footprint, conserving resources, and adopting eco-friendly technologies.

Community Engagement

We actively engage with communities where we operate, supporting local initiatives, fostering partnerships, and contributing to social welfare programs.

Employee Volunteering

We encourage and support our employees' participation in volunteer activities and community service initiatives.

Philanthropy

We allocate resources towards charitable causes and organizations that align with our values and contribute positively to society.

4. Implementation
Responsibility

The CSR Committee, comprised of representatives from various departments, is responsible for overseeing the implementation and periodic review of this policy.

Integration

CSR considerations are integrated into our business strategy, decision-making processes, and day-to-day operations.

Reporting

We provide regular updates and reports on our CSR activities and performance to stakeholders, ensuring transparency and accountability.

5. Compliance
Legal Compliance

We comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and international standards related to CSR in every jurisdiction where we operate.

Continuous Improvement

We continuously evaluate and improve our CSR initiatives, policies, and performance based on feedback and evolving best practices.

6. Communication
Internal Communication

We communicate our CSR objectives, initiatives, and achievements internally to raise awareness and foster a culture of social responsibility among employees.

External Communication

We engage with external stakeholders, including customers, suppliers, partners, and the wider community, to share information about our CSR activities and encourage collaboration.

7. Resources
Allocation

Adequate resources, including financial, human, and technological, are allocated to support the effective implementation of CSR initiatives.

8. Review and Update
Review

This CSR Policy is periodically reviewed and updated to reflect changing societal expectations, business priorities, and emerging sustainability challenges.

9. Adoption

This CSR Policy is adopted and endorsed by ABARIS HEALTHCARE PVT. LTD.
